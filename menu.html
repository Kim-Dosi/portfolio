01

Common

(2020)
Editorial/Identity Design, Branding, Photography
170*240mm
<Common> is a lifestyle magazine for all women that does not differentiate women according to their stance but deals with realistic stories that we are facing in everyday lives through various contents including interviews, columns, and essays. The magazine was produced by four editors who met at the film festival and published through Tumblebug crowdfunding.
<Common> is currently stocked in ‘Egg Studio in Whistle Stop’ in Iksan, ‘The Pollack’, ‘Ghost Books’ in Daegu, ‘Bookstore Ujoosonyeon(Space boy)’ in Yongin, and ‘Storagebookandfilm Gangnam’, ‘House Books’, ‘Classic Bookstore’, ‘Nowafter Books’ in Seoul.
02

Themis’ Scale

(2020)
Object/Graphic Design
500*200*390mm(Object), 80*150mm(Printed Stuff)
<Themis’ Scale> is a participation work in the exhibition <Infection and Immunity>, which was held at the Doosung Paper Gallery in Seoul from November 5 to 26, 2020. In an exhibition on social infection and tools to immunize individuals from it, the work defined one of the forms of social infection as ‘consumption infection’ and was produced as a means of protecting individuals from a capitalistic(consumer) society.
<Themis’ Scale> is an object that stamps on the stigma on the floor with one side of scale down when consumption exceeds a user-defined limit. Balance/imbalance of the scale and therefore motility were used as the main motif.
