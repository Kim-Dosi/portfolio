Cinematic Seoul

(2020)
Web Design, Photography
1439*971px
<Cinematic Seoul> is a project to archive film locations in Seoul. Although one film was made into a dramatic film, it also works as a documentary about space. It reveals what has changed or hasn’t changed between now and the time when the film was been shot. The exploration uncovered the hidden layer of Seoul using film as a ‘clue’. The project was completed in a form of a website including photography and essays.
